Welcome to our Memory Game

  • there are 5 options
  • the easiest to solve is 4x4 (16 fields in total, 8 pairs of signs)
  • the most difficult is 12x12 (144 fields in total, 72 pairs of signs)
  • for each option, according to its difficulty to solve, you have a limited time
  • the game is simple, you open one field of your choice, a sign is hidden behind it
  • then you open another field
  • if you hit the sign, the fields remain open
  • in case you did not hit the same sign, the fields are closed
  • you need to open all fields
  • if you have overestimated your capabilities, press the button "Stop and back" and choose the option with fewer fields
